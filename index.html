    An NFPT (Non-Fungible Physical Token) is a type of physical asset designed for collecting and trading.
    Part of the token represents ownership or proof of authenticity. The NFPT is meant to be a unique item.

      The NFPT may have multiple digital tokens associated with it:

       Current owner (generally abstracted)
       Size
       Prior owner  (generally abstracted)
       Ownership chain  (generally abstracted)
       Estimated market value
       Issued date
       Image
       Media
       Type
       Creator
       Modified date
       Last selling price
       Offered price (bid)
       Asking price (ask)
       Selling price history
       Copyright
